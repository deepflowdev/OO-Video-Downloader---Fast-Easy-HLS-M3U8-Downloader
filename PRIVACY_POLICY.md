## 3. Core App Functionality & Permissions

### A. Media Storage & File Access
To fulfill its primary purpose, the app requires access to your device's storage (`READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE`, `WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE`, and `READ_MEDIA_VIDEO/IMAGES` for newer Android versions). This allows the app to:
*   Save extracted video and audio files directly to your storage.
*   Scan and list downloaded media for internal playback.
*   Synchronize with the system MediaStore so files appear in your gallery.

### B. Foreground Service (Data Sync & Processing)
The app utilizes a **Foreground Service** (`FOREGROUND_SERVICE_DATA_SYNC`) to manage active downloads and media processing (such as M3U8 segment merging). This ensures that your tasks are completed reliably even if you minimize the app or the screen turns off. A persistent notification is shown while this service is active.
